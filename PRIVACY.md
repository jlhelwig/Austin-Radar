# Austin Radar: Privacy Policy
*Last Updated: April 10, 2026*

## 1. Data Collection
- **Precise Location**: Austin Radar collects precise location data to provide real-time alerts for nearby celebrity sightings, speakeasy discoveries, and time-sensitive happy hour events.
- **Background Location**: If enabled, the app monitors your location in the background to provide these alerts even when the app is closed. This is user-configurable and includes an automatic 4-hour kill switch.
- **Media**: Photos uploaded to "Hidden Gems" are compressed and stored securely. We do not collect metadata (EXIF) from your photos.

## 2. Data Usage
- **Anonymity**: Community "Hidden Gems" are shared anonymously. No usernames, profile pictures, or upload timestamps are visible to other users.
- **Zero Sharing**: We do not sell your location data or personal information to third parties.

## 3. Account & Storage
- **Google OAuth**: We use Google for authentication. We only store your unique User ID to provision your private data storage on our backend.
- **Data Deletion**: Users can request full deletion of their account and all submitted "Gems" at any time.

## 4. Third-Party Services
- **Sentry**: Used for silent error reporting and performance monitoring.
- **Supabase/Firebase**: Used for secure data storage and geospatial querying.
